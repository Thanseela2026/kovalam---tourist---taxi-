About Kovalam Tourist Taxi

Kovalam Tourist Taxi provides safe, reliable and comfortable taxi service across Kerala. Based in Kovalam, we offer airport transfers, local sightseeing, outstation trips and Kerala tour packages.

Our aim is to provide a smooth and memorable travel experience for families, couples and tourists visiting Kerala.

About us

Experienced Kerala Tourist Driver

Born and raised in the beautiful coastal town of Kovalam, I know Kerala's roads, attractions, and hidden gems like a local.With 20+ years of professional driving experience, I am dedicated to providing safe, comfortable, and reliable transportation for every guest.
